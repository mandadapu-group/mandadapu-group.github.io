About Us

We do interdisciplinary research at the intersection of statistical and continuum mechanics. We seek to understand phenomena at different length and time scales by developing theories and associated numerical methods.

We are part of the Pitzer Center for theoretical chemistry, located in Gilman Hall at the University of California, Berkeley. Group members have a variety of backgrounds—including chemical engineering, mechanical engineering, physics, and chemistry.

This site is meant to be a resource containing all relevant research information. For each of the various projects, you can find a summary of our results, as well as papers and source code.
